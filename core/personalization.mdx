## Home

On your home page, you have easy access to your Views and Bookmarks. Additionally, we surface current data on Ethereum such as:

- Latest transaction
- Latest Block
- Date range we've indexed

## Sidebar

Your sidebar gives you access to the Home page, your Views and Bookmarks. It's also home to your settings and other helpful tools like contacting us.

## Account Settings

In your account settings, you can:

- Edit your name
- Email address
- Configure your authentication preferences
- Log out or delete your account
