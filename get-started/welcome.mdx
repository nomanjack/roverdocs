Rover is an all-in-one on-chain information engine that enables you to search, monitor, and aggregate blockchain activity in real-time, transforming complex data into intuitive, actionable insights for everyone.

![Rover Dashboard](/figma-assets/2ba7b232b895e71ef041b1e90753684f6711124a.png)

We're the blockchain's only filter-based search engine. Ask complex questions and get instant answers, it's that simple.

While traditional block explorers show you data, we help you find exactly what you're looking for. We've kept all the core features you expect from an explorer, added powerful filtering capabilities, and sprinkled in some AI magic to make blockchain data truly accessible.

## Status & Data Coverage

The beta release is focused on Ethereum. We offer 1 month of historical data and we live stream loads every 2 minutes with new blocks being mined and finalized.
